Instruments

Guitar

The guitar is a versatile stringed instrument with a wooden body and a long neck featuring frets for finger placement. Its six strings produce a wide range of sounds, making it a popular choice in various music genres.

Piano

The piano is a versatile keyboard instrument with a wide range of keys, typically 88, that produce sound when pressed, striking strings connected to a soundboard. It's a cornerstone of classical, jazz, and contemporary music, offering dynamic expression and harmonic richness.

Love Story

"Love Story" by Taylor Swift is a romantic country-pop song that tells the tale of forbidden love inspired by William Shakespeare's "Romeo and Juliet." With catchy melodies and heartfelt lyrics, it narrates the journey of two lovers overcoming obstacles to be together, evoking themes of destiny, passion, and enduring love.

Enjoy the Silence

"Enjoy the Silence" is a song by Depeche Mode. Released in 1990 as a single from their album "Violator," the song became a massive hit worldwide, topping charts in multiple countries. Its haunting melody, evocative lyrics, and iconic music video contributed to its enduring popularity and cemented it as one of Depeche Mode's signature songs.

Back to You

"Back to You" is a song by Selena Gomez that was released in 2018 as a single for the second season of the Netflix series "13 Reasons Why," which Gomez co-produced. The song features a catchy melody with upbeat pop production, and its lyrics reflect themes of love, longing, and the complexity of relationships.
